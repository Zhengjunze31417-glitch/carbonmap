How it works & limitations
Models
Above-Ground Carbon
R² = 0.67
Model XGBoost regression on log₁₊(AGC)
Features Sentinel-2 B4/B8/B11, NDVI, EVI, NDMI, NIRv, DEM, slope, mean annual temperature & precipitation (seasonal)
Output MgC ha⁻¹ (tonnes carbon per hectare)
⚠ Filter: NDVI > 0.2 & NDBI < 0
Soil Organic Carbon
R² = 0.34
Model XGBoost trained on WoSIS global field measurements
Features 17 spectral (bare-soil composites), topographic, and climate variables
Output g kg⁻¹ (grams carbon per kg of soil)
⚠ Filter: NDVI < 0.25 & NBR2 < 0.07 · RMSE = 42.7 g kg⁻¹
Data & limitations
📡 Sentinel-2 at 10–20 m resolution via Google Earth Engine. Monthly median composites, 2019–2025.
☁ Cloud-contaminated months may return null values, especially in tropical or monsoon regions.
🌍 Both models were trained on global datasets; predictions in under-represented regions may carry additional bias.
🗺 Historical basemap via Esri Wayback — update frequency varies by region. Some areas have only 1–2 captures per year.
